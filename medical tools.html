To use medical devices safely, Know how your device works.

blood glucose meter

Steps for Use Blood Glucose Meter

  • Wash your hands with soap and water. Dry thoroughly.
  • Prepare the meter, test strip, and lancet.
  • Insert a test strip into the meter.
  • Prick your fingertip with the lancet to get a small drop of blood.
  • Touch the blood drop to the test strip.
  • Wait for the reading on the meter display.
  • Record your result if needed.
  • Dispose of the lancet and strip safely.

Blood Pressure Device

Steps for Use Blood Pressure Device

  • Sit calmly for 5 minutes before measuring.
  • Place your arm on a table at heart level.
  • Wrap the cuff snugly around your upper arm.
  • Turn on the device and start the measurement.
  • Stay still and quiet while it inflates and measures.
  • Read your results on the screen.
  • Record your reading if needed.
  • Remove the cuff and turn off the device.

Digital Termometer

Steps for Use Digital Termometer

  • Turn on the thermometer.
  • Place it correctly:
    1. Under the tongue (oral)
    2. Under the armpit (axillary)
    3. In the ear or on the forehead (if designed for those areas)
  • Wait for the beep or signal that the reading is done.
  • Read the temperature on the display.
  • Clean the thermometer as instructed.
  • Turn it off and store safely.
