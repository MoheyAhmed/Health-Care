What is Blood?

Main Components of Blood

  • Red Blood Cells (RBCs)
    Carry oxygen from your lungs to the rest of your body and give blood its red color.
  • White Blood Cells (WBCs)
    Help fight infections and protect your immune system.
  • Platelets
    Help your blood clot to stop bleeding when you’re injured.
  • Plasma
    The liquid part of blood, mostly water, carrying hormones, nutrients, and waste products.

Blood Types:

  • Four main blood types: A, B, AB, O
  • Each can be Rh-positive (+) or Rh-negative (−)
  • Knowing your blood type is important for safe blood transfusions.
